This year, I’m taking on a new challenge: my first international ride. I’ve grappled with the carbon footprint from flying, but I’m committed to mitigating it by choosing greener airlines and investing in carbon credits. This journey will be both physical and spiritual, and I’ll share my experiences, struggles with climate guilt, and impactful efforts on my Facebook page.

Why Slovenia? It’s one of the world’s most sustainable countries. Traveling there with Climate Ride will allow me to meet passionate climate activists and learn from their successes.

My Charities: All direct donations will support Climate Ride, a nonprofit organizing adventures to protect our planet. Additionally, I’ll match donations over $250, directing them to Save The Redwood League, dedicated to preserving California’s redwood forests.

Funding Goal: $4,500+ Last year, I raised over $6,000 for Save The Redwood League. I hope to match or exceed this generosity.

About My Charities:

  • Climate Ride: Mobilizes people through biking, running, and hiking events to support sustainability and environmental causes.
  • Save The Redwoods League: Works to restore and protect California’s redwood forests, partnering with local communities for sustainable access.

Incentives:

  • $25+ Personal thank you card
  • $100 Personal thank you + honey from Slovenia’s Carniolan honey bee
  • $200 Matching donation to Save The Redwoods League
  • $500 Ticket to Save The Redwood League’s Take Me to The Trees event on May 18th in Presidio, SF

The Ride: We’ll journey from Ljubljana to the Alps, crossing into Austria and down to Lake Bled, tackling epic climbs like Vrsic Pass.

My Team: We Bled for This – a group of seasoned female bikers.

Plea: We’re at a critical point for climate action. Support can come in many forms: contacting representatives, volunteering, or donating. If you believe in me, Save The Redwoods League, or Climate Ride’s mission, please donate to my ride. Follow my journey on Facebook and Instagram for updates and sustainable living tips.

Thank you for your support!
