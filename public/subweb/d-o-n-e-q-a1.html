What are the main causes of climate change?
  1. Burning fossil fuels (coal, oil, and natural gas) for energy, which releases greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide (CO2).
  2. Deforestation, which reduces the number of trees that can absorb CO2 from the atmosphere.
  3. Industrial activities that emit greenhouse gases and other pollutants.
  4. Agricultural practices , especially livestock farming, which produce methane (CH4).
  5. Waste management, including landfills, which release methane as organic waste decomposes.
