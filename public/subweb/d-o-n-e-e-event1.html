With a humanistic message for the National Green Environment. Over the past 12 years, the Program has been deployed to call on all people to work together to protect their health and living environment, promote a healthy lifestyle for the community, and aim for a greener - cleaner - more beautiful life in the future.

The Green Environment Program focuses on mobilizing all humanity to increase activities associated with nature, while sharing and continuing the call to protect the earth and protect harmonious and sustainable relationships. stability between humans and nature. Some environmental cleaning activities at Cat Ba Island - Hai Phong, My Khe Beach, Da Nang with the message "Tourism development associated with environmental protection". Cycling to spread the message of action for the environment in Hoi An, walking and picking up trash at Vung Tau beach, Or running for the environment at Hoan Kiem walking street in Hanoi...

In 2023, with the message Green Living, propagate to students from 7-15 years old to raise awareness and action to protect the environment, contribute to changing awareness about the use of recycling, and guide students to have Positive thoughts, actions, and actions to protect the environment and natural resources. Through the Green Ambassador contest, we also found 35 outstanding children who are inspirational messengers for the environment to call on future preschoolers to be clearly aware of the current state of the living environment in the community, and Take action to protect the environment, contributing to a better community life.

In 2024, the Program will be held in many provinces/cities across the country such as Hai Phong, Khanh Hoa, Ho Chi Minh City... continuing to contribute to raising awareness of the community about environmental protection awareness and responsibility. ; serve as a bridge connecting businesses, joining hands to protect the environment, contributing to implementing the Party's policies and State legal policies on environmental protection.

The annual "For National Green Environment" Program activities are organized in combination with many diverse and rich activities, ensuring practicality, focus, and focus in accordance with local requirements.
